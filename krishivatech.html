Project Overview

Krishiva Tech is a modern technology solutions company offering services such as web development, mobile app development, UI/UX design, and digital marketing. The goal of this project was to design a conversion-focused landing page that clearly communicates the brand’s expertise, services, and credibility while encouraging users to get in touch or request a quote.

Project Goals

  • Design a modern and professional landing page that reflects the company’s expertise in technology and digital solutions
  • RClearly communicate the brand value proposition within the first few seconds of user interaction
  • Present services in a structured and easy-to-understand manner
  • Build trust and credibility through testimonials, portfolio showcase, and global user statistics
  • Align the design with modern SaaS and agency UI standards
  • Ensure a responsive and accessible layout for consistent experience across devices

    • Design Approach

    The landing page was designed with a user-first and conversion-focused approach. The layout emphasizes clear visual hierarchy, concise messaging, and strategically placed CTAs to guide users from awareness to action.
    Key sections such as services, technology stack, portfolio, and testimonials were structured to improve scannability and build trust. Clean spacing, modern typography, and consistent color usage were applied to create a professional and approachable brand experience across all devices.

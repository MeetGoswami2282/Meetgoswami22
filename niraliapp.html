UI/UX Designer

Project Overview

This project is a B2C e-commerce mobile application designed for purchasing commercial kitchen and food machinery.
The app covers the entire user journey, from product discovery and category browsing to cart management, checkout, order tracking, invoices, and account management.

Project Goals

  • Design a smooth end-to-end shopping experience for machinery purchases
  • Make complex information (GST, invoices, order status) easy to understand
  • Reduce friction during cart, checkout, and payment
  • Enable users to track orders, download invoices, and manage accounts
  • Create a scalable design system that can support future features
  • Maintain a clean, modern, and trustworthy visual style

    • The goal was to create a clean, intuitive, and scalable mobile experience that simplifies buying high-value machines while maintaining transparency in pricing, taxes, and order status.

    Design Approach

  • Followed a user-first approach, mapping screens based on real e-commerce flows
  • Kept layouts minimal and scannable for faster decision-making
  • Designed action-focused CTAs for checkout, order tracking, and payments
  • Maintained visual consistency across product listing, details, cart, and account screens
  • Optimized for mobile usability, including thumb-friendly interactions
  • Maintain a clean, modern, and trustworthy visual style
